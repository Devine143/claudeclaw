# ClaudeClaw — Image Generation Prompts

Reference file for generating Despicable Me minion agent images.
All prompts are copy-paste ready.

---

## Style Guide

- **Character:** Despicable Me minion, Pixar CGI style 3D render
- **Framing:** Head to shins, character fills most of the frame
- **Background:** Dark solid/gradient, no scene — just mood colour
- **Lighting:** Warm cinematic, soft shadows
- **Each agent gets:** Unique overalls colour, unique background gradient, role-specific props
- **Avoid:** Full body with lots of ground, wide shots, busy scenes, landscape orientation
